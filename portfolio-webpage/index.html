About Me

I'm passionate about staying up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies in the industry, and I'm always seeking new challenges to help me grow and improve as a software engineer.

Thank you for taking the time to check out my portfolio, and please feel free to contact me with any questions or opportunities!

Past Experiences

As a Junior Developer at Pensano Devs, I had the opportunity to work on a variety of challenging and exciting projects. I gained valuable experience in Ruby and JavaScript, and had the chance to collaborate closely with senior developers to contribute to the development of complex software solutions. One of my most memorable projects was a web application that I worked on with a team of other developers - I was responsible for designing and implementing several key features, and I learned a lot from the feedback and guidance of my colleagues. Working at Pensano Devs helped me develop my technical skills and taught me the importance of collaboration and communication in a fast-paced, dynamic work environment.

Before that, I worked 2 years as an HR Manager at HR Global. I was responsible for overseeing all aspects of HR operations, from recruitment and onboarding to performance management and employee relations. I led a team of HR professionals and worked closely with leadership to align HR strategy with the broader goals of the organization. One of my proudest achievements in this role was implementing a new employee development program that helped to improve retention and engagement across the organization. I also enjoyed working on diversity and inclusion initiatives, and was pleased to see the positive impact they had on employee morale and overall company culture. Working in HR taught me the importance of balancing the needs of individuals with the needs of the organization, and gave me a deep appreciation for the human side of the workplace.
